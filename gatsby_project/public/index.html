Espresso Cheesecake

Ingredients

Crust

  • 2 cups oreo crumbs

  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Cheesecake

  • 3 packages cream cheese at room temperature, 8 oz each, I like to use full fat

  • 1 cup sugar

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 1/3 cup brewed espresso

  • 3 eggs

Chocolate Ganache

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

  • 1 cup chocolate chips (I used semi-sweet)
